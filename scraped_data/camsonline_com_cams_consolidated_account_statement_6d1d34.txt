CAMS (Computer Age Management Services) - Consolidated Account Statement (CAS):

What is CAS?
A Consolidated Account Statement (CAS) is a single document that shows all your mutual fund holdings across all AMCs and folios linked to your PAN. It is generated by RTAs (Registrar and Transfer Agents) like CAMS and KFintech.

How to Request CAS from CAMS:
1. Visit www.camsonline.com
2. Navigate to Investor Services > Statements > Consolidated Account Statement
3. Enter your registered email address or PAN
4. Select the statement period (monthly, half-yearly, or custom range)
5. Submit the request
6. CAS will be emailed to your registered email as a password-protected PDF
7. The PDF password is typically your PAN in lowercase

What CAS Includes:
- All mutual fund holdings across all AMCs (HDFC MF, SBI MF, ICICI MF, etc.)
- Transaction summary for the selected period
- Portfolio valuation as on the statement date
- Folio-wise and scheme-wise breakdown
- Cost of acquisition details for tax computation
- Dividend/IDCW history

CAMS as HDFC MF Registrar:
CAMS is the Registrar and Transfer Agent for HDFC Mutual Fund. All HDFC MF account-related services including statements, transactions, and account changes are processed through CAMS.

SEBI mandates that a CAS be sent to investors monthly (if there are transactions) or at least once every six months.
