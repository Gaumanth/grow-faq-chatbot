# Sample Q&A — Groww MF FAQ Chatbot (HDFC AMC)

Below are 10 sample queries with the expected style of answer from the assistant.

---

### Q1: What is the expense ratio of HDFC Top 100 Fund?

**A:** The expense ratio of HDFC Top 100 Fund (Direct Plan) is 1.04% as per the latest factsheet. The Regular Plan has an expense ratio of 1.78%. Last updated from sources: March 2025

**Source:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/mutual-funds/equity-fund/hdfc-top-100-fund

---

### Q2: What is the exit load for HDFC Flexi Cap Fund?

**A:** HDFC Flexi Cap Fund charges an exit load of 1% if units are redeemed within 1 year from the date of allotment. There is no exit load after 1 year. Last updated from sources: March 2025

**Source:** https://groww.in/mutual-funds/hdfc-flexi-cap-fund-direct-plan-growth

---

### Q3: What is the lock-in period for HDFC ELSS Tax Saver Fund?

**A:** HDFC ELSS Tax Saver Fund has a mandatory lock-in period of 3 years as per SEBI regulations for all ELSS funds. Each SIP installment has its own 3-year lock-in from its allotment date. There is no exit load on redemption after the lock-in period. Last updated from sources: March 2025

**Source:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/mutual-funds/equity-fund/hdfc-elss-tax-saver

---

### Q4: What is the minimum SIP amount for HDFC Mid-Cap Opportunities Fund?

**A:** The minimum SIP amount for HDFC Mid-Cap Opportunities Fund is Rs 100. SIP frequency options include monthly and quarterly. Last updated from sources: March 2025

**Source:** https://groww.in/mutual-funds/hdfc-mid-cap-opportunities-fund-direct-plan-growth

---

### Q5: What is the riskometer category for HDFC equity funds?

**A:** All four HDFC equity schemes (Top 100, Flexi Cap, ELSS Tax Saver, and Mid-Cap Opportunities Fund) are categorised as "Very High" risk on the SEBI riskometer. This means the principal invested is subject to very high risk and significant fluctuations. Last updated from sources: March 2025

**Source:** https://www.sebi.gov.in/legal/circulars/oct-2017/categorization-and-rationalization-of-mutual-fund-schemes_36199.html

---

### Q6: What is the benchmark for HDFC Top 100 Fund?

**A:** The benchmark index for HDFC Top 100 Fund is NIFTY 100 TRI (Total Return Index). TRI includes dividend reinvestment for more accurate comparison with fund returns. Last updated from sources: March 2025

**Source:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/mutual-funds/equity-fund/hdfc-top-100-fund

---

### Q7: How do I download my capital gains statement on Groww?

**A:** On Groww, go to your account, navigate to the "Reports" section, and select "Capital Gains Report" or "Tax P&L". Choose the financial year and download the report in PDF format for tax filing purposes. Last updated from sources: March 2025

**Source:** https://groww.in/p/capital-gains-statement/

### Q9: What is a Consolidated Account Statement (CAS)?

**A:** A CAS is a single combined statement of all your mutual fund investments across all AMCs linked to your PAN. You can request it from CAMS (camsonline.com) or KFintech (mfs.kfintech.com) and it is delivered to your registered email. Last updated from sources: March 2025

**Source:** https://www.camsonline.com/Investors/Statements/Consolidated-Account-Statement
